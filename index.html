Temporal In-Context Fine-Tuning
for Versatile Control of Video Diffusion Models

Kinam Kim*, Junha Hyung*, Jaegul Choo
KAIST AI
Code arXiv

Abstract

We introduce Temporal In-Context Fine-Tuning (TIC-FT), a simple training-free sampling guidance method for enhancing transformer-based video diffusion models. TIC-FT employs an implicit weak model via self-perturbation, avoiding the need for external models or additional training. By selectively skipping spatiotemporal layers, TIC-FT produces an aligned, degraded version of the original model to boost sample quality without compromising diversity or dynamic degree.
